# Addiction & Hunger System — Implementation Suggestions

**Status:** Core system design (v1)  
**Related Systems:** Modular Text System, Compound System, Character State Machine

---

## 1. Overview

This document defines the **Addiction** and **Hunger** systems tied to Sophia’s compounds and the broader game. These two systems are **related but separate**:

- **Hunger** = General desire to eat and be fed.
- **Addiction** = Specific craving for food and compounds **given by the player**.

Addiction should feel meaningful and slightly punishing without becoming overwhelming. Hunger should feel reactive and alive, especially at higher levels.

---

## 2. Core Rules

- Compounds must be delivered through **food or drink** (never directly).
- Addiction is **player-specific** — girls crave *you* feeding them.
- Hunger and addiction are related but not 1:1. Many things can increase hunger; addiction specifically amplifies it and unlocks worse behaviors.
- Higher addiction levels unlock **more deranged and desperate** hunger behaviors.
- There should be a **Stage 3 drug** that can reduce addiction back down to **Mild** (but not remove it entirely).

---

## 3. Addiction System

### Addiction Levels

| Level     | Name        | Description                              |
|-----------|-------------|------------------------------------------|
| 0         | None        | No addiction                             |
| 1         | Mild        | Light craving                            |
| 2         | Moderate    | Noticeable dependency                    |
| 3         | Severe      | Strong, difficult-to-ignore craving      |
| 4         | Dependent   | Deep, near-constant need                 |

### How Addiction is Gained

- Taking addictive compounds (Pleasure Enhancer, Weight Gain Potion, Craving Inducer, Intentional Addiction Compound, etc.).
- Repeated heavy feeding while already at Moderate+ addiction.
- Some evolved forms and high corruption can contribute.

### Effects by Level

| Level      | Hunger Impact                     | Withdrawal Risk | Behavioral Changes                          | Visibility      |
|------------|-----------------------------------|-----------------|---------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| None       | Normal                            | None            | Normal                                      | None            |
| Mild       | Slightly increased                | Low             | Slightly more food-focused                  | Very Low        |
| Moderate   | Noticeably hungrier               | Medium          | Starts asking/begging more often            | Low             |
| Severe     | Very strong hunger                | High            | More desperate, possible manipulation       | Medium          |
| Dependent  | Near-constant intense hunger      | Very High       | Deranged / aggressive craving states        | High            |

### Withdrawal

If a girl at **Moderate+** addiction is neglected for too long, she enters **Withdrawal**.

- **Primary symptom**: **Aggression** (irritability, short temper, lashing out).
- Other effects: reduced mood, slower gains, refusing certain interactions.

### Curing Addiction

- **Stage 3** unlocks a drug that can reduce addiction back down to **Mild**.
- Addiction cannot be fully removed once acquired.

### Visibility

Addiction should have **very low visibility**:
- No obvious meter.
- Only visual cue: subtle color shift on the girl’s card (light red/orange tint).
- Most information should come through behavior and interrupting scenes.

---

## 4. Hunger System

Hunger exists independently of addiction. Many sources can raise it (normal feeding, compounds, corruption, evolved forms, etc.).

### Hunger Tiers

| Tier | Name                | Description                          |
|------|---------------------|--------------------------------------|
| 0    | Normal              | Regular appetite                     |
| 1    | Increased           | Clearly thinking about food more     |
| 2    | High                | Very hungry                          |
| 3    | Craving Hunger      | Strong, difficult-to-ignore hunger   |
| 4    | Starving Hunger     | Desperate, near-frantic hunger       |

**Important Rule**: Tiers 3 and 4 (**Craving Hunger** and **Starving Hunger**) are **only available** when the girl has **Moderate+ addiction**. These represent the “deranged” tiers.

### Deranged Hunger Behaviors (Addiction Tier 3+)

When a girl reaches Tier 3 or 4, she can exhibit more unhinged behaviors such as:
- Actively seeking the player out
- Intense, desperate begging
- Getting aggressive or emotional if denied
- Trying to manipulate or guilt the player
- Making needy or desperate sounds while eating
- Looking almost worshipful or dazed while being fed

---

## 5. Interruption Mechanic (“Knock at the Door”)

When the player tries to advance the week or spend an action point:

- If any girl is in **Craving Hunger (Tier 3)**, **Starving Hunger (Tier 4)**, or deep in **Withdrawal**, there is a chance of an interrupting scene.
- These scenes usually start with lines like:
  - “There’s a knock at your door…”
  - “You hear someone outside…”
- These interruptions force the player to deal with the girl immediately and can cost time, resources, or relationship progress.

This is the main way high addiction creates **real ongoing friction**.

---

## 6. Integration with Modular Text System

All hunger and addiction text should go through the **Modular Text System**.

### Recommended New Modules

| Module Name                  | Purpose                                              | Example Output |
|-----------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|----------------|
| `hunger.desc`               | Describes current hunger level                       | “She’s clearly starving.” |
| `hunger.addictedDesc`       | Addiction-flavored hunger description                | “She’s starving for anything you’ll give her.” |
| `eating.style`              | How she eats right now                               | “She devours the food like she’s afraid it’ll be taken away.” |
| `talk.hungryCoda`           | Hunger-related dialogue additions                    | “She keeps glancing at you with that hungry look.” |
| `scene.hungerInterrupt`     | Interrupting scenes when advancing time              | “There’s a knock at your door. She looks almost frantic.” |

### Recommended Lexicon Additions

Add these dictionaries to `lexicon.js`:

- `HUNGER_WORDS`
- `ADDICTED_HUNGER_WORDS`
- `EATING_STYLE`
- `ADDICTED_EATING`

These should support both normal hunger and the more deranged behaviors at higher addiction levels.

---

## 7. Compound Interaction Rules

- All of Sophia’s compounds must be delivered through **food or drink**.
- Some compounds have addictive properties (especially Pleasure Enhancers, Weight Gain Potions, Craving Inducer, and the Stage 3 Intentional Addiction Compound).
- **Craving Inducer** should be **strong but short-term**. It can push girls into higher hunger tiers even at lower addiction levels.
- **Stage 3** includes a drug that can reduce addiction back to **Mild**.

---

## 8. Summary of Key Rules

- Hunger and addiction are **related but separate**.
- Addiction is **player-specific** (girls crave food from *you*).
- Compounds must go through food.
- Higher addiction unlocks **more deranged hunger behaviors**.
- Withdrawal causes **aggression**.
- There is a **Stage 3 drug** that can reduce addiction back to Mild.
- High hunger/addiction states can trigger **interrupting scenes** when advancing time.
- All text should be handled through the **Modular Text System**.
